Frequently Asked Questions

?

What exactly is BG?

BG is a platform designed to help everyday people grow money over time using automated strategies. It's not about trading all day or guessing markets, it's about letting systems do the heavy lifting while you stay hands-off.

?

Is this trading? Will I have to watch charts all day?

No. This is not day trading. You're not glued to screens or making constant decisions. Everything is designed to be automated so you're not babysitting it.

?

How much money do I need to start?

The current minimum to get started is $300. That said, people come in at different levels based on what they're comfortable with. Some start at $300, others choose $500, $1,000, or more.

?

Is this a get-rich-quick thing?

No, and that's actually a good thing. BG is about consistent growth over time, not overnight miracles. People who do best are the ones who think long-term.

?

Do I need experience with crypto or investing to get started?

Nope. Most people who get started have zero background in crypto or investing. BG is built for beginners, with tools and guidance that make the process simple and straightforward.

?

How does the compound growth part work?

Compounding simply means your gains can build on top of previous gains over time. Instead of pulling money out constantly, many people choose to let it grow and stack; slow, steady and intentional.

?

Is my money locked in? Can I withdraw?

You're in control of your funds. Withdrawals are available and there are no long-term lockups forcing you to stay in if you don't want to.

?

Do I need to recruit or build a team?

No. You can use BG purely as a customer. Sharing is optional, never required.
